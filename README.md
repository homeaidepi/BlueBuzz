# BlueBuzz
Never leave your phone at home, work, at a restaurant, in the car or your friends place ever again!

The only app that will buzz your Apple Watch when you are getting too far away from your iPhone and doesn't rely solely on Bluetooth. This is more than just a Bluetooth range app. You can customize the settings within the iPhone app so you receive the alert how you want, that works for you and your current environment. Set it 100-1000 feet away if you like. With distance, location & timing settings along with real-time cloud-based processing, your iPhone and Apple Watch will always be nearby. phone watch distance notification, location notification, separation alert, wearable alert, phone distance alert, watch phone alert, phone watch alert, phone watch alarm, phone distance alarm, watch phone alarm, blue buzz,
