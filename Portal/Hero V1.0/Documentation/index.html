B) Using the Themebuilder - top

Here're some steps of how to use our Themebuilder:

1. -With the 'video" tab you can add a video (if you have bought a video mail). How it works will be explained in the item description.
2. -With the tab "Change Header Images" you can adjust background images by entering a valid URL link.
3. -With the "Change Pattern" tab you can adjust the pattern colours in the body layout.
4. -With the tab "Change Styles" you can adjust all colours like text and backgrounds.



5. -With the Duplicate and Delete buttons you can duplicate or remove modules.



6. -When you hover over an image or text you see a pencil icon. When you click on this you can adjust or replace the text or image.
7. -With images you see a cross icon, you can remove the image with this.




8. -When you have adjusted/made your template you click on "Export Layout" and then export the file as HTML, MailChimp or Campaign Monitor.



9. -Then you send the template via an Email Platform.

E) MailChimp Support - top

1. Visit MailChimps website and login (righttop page).
2. Navigate to 'My Templates'.
3. Click on 'Create Template'.
4. Click 'Import HTML' and browse to the MailChimp index.html file located in your download folder.
5. Name your Template and click on 'Upload' and you're done!

Be sure to have all your images hosted on your own server. In example: 'http://www.yourwebsite.com/images/logo.jpg'

F) Gradients - top

Gradients aren't editable in our Themebuilder. As css gradients are poorly supported we created gradients with images. You are able to change the gradients simply by replacing the gradient image in your /images/ folder.
